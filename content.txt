SERVICE CLUSTER 1: CORPORATE SUPPORT SOLUTIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Service: Sponsorship & Government Relations (GR)
Scope: Lawful residency and work authorization for personnel; comprehensive legal residency services to secure legal status for clients.
Includes: Registration and establishment of work files with relevant ministries and government agencies, renewals, transfers, status changes, document attestation, and managing interactions with official entities.
Outputs: Permit schedules, status reports, and compliance documentation.

Service: Housing & Accommodation
Scope: Sourcing, leasing, and management of accommodation across prime and desirable locations throughout Kuwait and the region.
Includes: Compounds and apartments in various sizes; extensive amenities including swimming pools, 24/7 security, gyms, and pools; housekeeping; utilities; preventive maintenance; helpdesk; flexible options tailored to meet diverse client needs.
Outputs: Occupancy plans, HSE briefs, asset lists, and maintenance logs.

Service: Vehicles & Fleet
Scope: Latest vehicle models including sedans, crossovers, SUVs, pickup trucks, VANs, and buses for short‑ and long‑term terms; options that best suit diverse client needs.
Includes: Drivers on request, preventive maintenance, replacement units, and telematics where required.
Outputs: Fleet register, maintenance schedule, incident logs, and usage reports.

Service: Driving Insurance
Scope: Driver and vehicle insurance arrangements.
Includes: Policy selection, documentation, renewals, and claims coordination.
Outputs: Active coverage records and renewal calendar.

Service: Medical Care & Private Healthcare
Scope: Access coordination to medical services for staff and dependents.
Includes: Appointment booking, documentation, and follow‑up.
Outputs: Visit logs and assistance records.

Service: Translation & Interpretation
Scope: Arabic–English translation for operational documents and on‑site interpretation.
Includes: Contracts, permits, HSE materials, signage, and briefings.
Outputs: Approved translations and version control.
